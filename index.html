FAQs about PO Wizard

Everything you should know about our platform

PO Wizard is an AI-powered software designed for production coordinators to automate the creation of purchase orders. It drastically reduces manual data entry, minimizes errors, and saves significant time during the production wrap process.

PO Wizard is built specifically for production coordinators, line producers, production accountants, and anyone in the film, television, or commercial production industry who deals with purchase orders and vendor management.

You can upload various documents like invoices, receipts, or deal memos. Our AI intelligently scans these documents, extracts the necessary information (like vendor details, line items, costs, and project codes), and populates a professional purchase order automatically.

PO Wizard is designed for flexible integration. It can export data in formats compatible with popular industry accounting software like Global Vista, Media Services, and QuickBooks, ensuring a smooth handoff to your accounting department.

Absolutely. We prioritize your data's security above all else. All data is encrypted both in transit (using TLS 1.2+) and at rest (using AES-256). Our infrastructure is built on world-class, secure cloud providers and undergoes regular security audits to ensure your sensitive production information remains confidential and protected.

Our AI can process a wide range of common file types, including PDF, JPEG, PNG, and TIFF. Whether it's a scanned receipt, a vendor invoice PDF, or a photo of a document taken on your phone, you can upload it directly. We are continuously working to expand support for more formats.

Yes. Our Pro and Enterprise plans are built for collaboration. You can invite other team members, such as producers or accountants, to your production. Features like the complete audit trail and status tracking ensure everyone is on the same page, creating a single source of truth for all purchase orders.

We offer different levels of support based on your plan. All users have access to our comprehensive documentation and video tutorials. The Free plan includes email support. Our Pro plan includes priority email support, and the Enterprise plan comes with a dedicated account manager and phone support to provide immediate assistance for any complex needs.
